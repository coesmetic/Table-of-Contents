Fruit

  • Carbohydrate Content: Fruit is generally higher in carbs, ranging from 10-30 grams per serving (typically 1 medium piece or 1 cup).
  • Low-Carb Fruits: Berries, melons, and citrus fruits are generally lower in carbs than bananas, apples, and grapes.
  • Considerations: Dried fruits are more concentrated in carbs and sugar due to dehydration, often containing 40-60 grams of carbs per serving.
