Beans

  • Carbohydrate Content: Beans are relatively high in carbs, with about 20-30 grams per cup of cooked beans (varies by type).
  • Types: Black beans, chickpeas, kidney beans, and lentils contain a mix of complex carbs, fiber, and some protein.
  • Net Carbs: Because of high fiber content, the net carbs are typically lower than total carbs, making beans suitable in moderation for low-carb diets.
