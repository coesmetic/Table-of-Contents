Bread

  • Carbohydrate Content: Bread varies widely in carb content; white and whole-wheat breads typically have around 12-20 grams per slice, while low-carb and “keto” breads contain 5 grams or less per slice.
  • Types: Whole grain and sprouted breads offer more fiber, which can lower net carbs slightly, while refined white breads generally provide fewer nutrients and more net carbs.
