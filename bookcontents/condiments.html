Condiments

  • Carbohydrate Content: Condiments vary widely; ketchup and barbecue sauce can contain 4-7 grams of carbs per tablespoon due to added sugars, while mustard, hot sauce, and mayonnaise often have minimal to no carbs.
  • Low-Carb Options: Sugar-free versions of ketchup, dressings, and sauces can reduce carb content significantly.
